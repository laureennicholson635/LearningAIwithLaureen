How it works

From manual to automated in four steps

Every engagement follows the same simple path: we find the work worth automating, build it, connect it, and keep it running. You stay in control the whole way.

1. Map your workflow

We walk through how a task actually gets done today — every click, hand-off, and copy-paste — and find where the hours go.

2. Build the automation

We build AI agents and automated workflows that do the repetitive parts, using tools like Make.com, Cloudflare Workers, and the Claude AI model family.

3. Connect your tools

The automation plugs into the software you already use — email, spreadsheets, your CRM, your billing system — so nothing has to change overnight.

4. Monitor & improve

We test it, document it, and (on the Monthly Partner plan) watch it, fix it, and keep adding new automations over time.

Common questions

AI workflow automation, answered

Short, direct answers to the questions business owners ask most. See the full FAQ for 18 more.

What is AI workflow automation consulting?

AI workflow automation consulting is a service that finds repetitive manual tasks in your business and replaces them with AI agents and automated workflows. The consultant maps your process, builds the automation, connects it to your existing tools, and maintains it so the work runs without constant human effort.

How much does it cost to automate my business with AI?

Nicholson AI Group has three options: a $497 one-time AI Starter assessment, a $2,500–$4,500 one-time Done-For-You Deployment, and a Monthly Partner plan from $1,500–$2,500 per month. You can start small and only move up when the savings are obvious. See full pricing.

What kind of businesses benefit most from AI automation?

Small and mid-size businesses with repetitive, rules-based work benefit most — medical billing offices, professional services firms, agencies, and operations teams that lose hours each week to data entry, follow-ups, scheduling, and email.

Do I need technical skills to use the automations you build?

No. The automations run in the background and connect to tools you already use. You see the results — tasks done, emails sent, reports delivered — without touching any code.
