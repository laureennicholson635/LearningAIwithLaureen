Pricing questions

What people ask before they buy

How much does AI workflow automation cost?

Nicholson AI Group has three price points: a $497 one-time Starter Assessment, a $2,500–$4,500 one-time Done-For-You Deployment, and a Monthly Partner plan from $1,500–$2,500 per month. There are no hidden fees and every project is quoted before work begins.

Are there any setup fees or hidden costs?

No. The price you're quoted is the price you pay. For Done-For-You Deployments, the exact figure within the range is confirmed in writing before any work starts, based on the number of steps and integrations.

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

No. The Starter Assessment and Done-For-You Deployment are one-time projects. The Monthly Partner plan is month-to-month and can be cancelled anytime.

Can I apply the Starter Assessment fee to a larger project?

Yes. If you move forward with a Done-For-You Deployment or Monthly Partner plan after an assessment, the $497 fee is credited toward that work.

What's the return on investment?

It depends on the task, but the math is simple: if a workflow takes your team five hours a week and you value that time at $30 an hour, that's about $7,800 a year. A one-time deployment that removes it typically pays for itself within months. We estimate your specific numbers during the free review.
