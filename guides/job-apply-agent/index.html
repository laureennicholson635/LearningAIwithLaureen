I want you to help me build a simple "job application assistant" — I am not a coder, so explain everything in plain English and walk me through it one step at a time. Wait for me to finish each step before giving me the next.

Here is what I want it to do:
1. Let me save my job search rules (remote only, minimum pay, keywords I care about, location).
2. Take a list of job postings I paste in, and tell me which ones match my rules and which to skip — and why.
